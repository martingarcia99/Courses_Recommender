Welcome on our lecture recommender system: Courses.

In this project work we use information about your study and interests for recommending you the courses that fit the best to you.



Idea.

We give you the possibility to dive deeper in the lecture information as well as give you an overview about the evaluation of the last semester. If the recommendation doesn’t fit, you can easily change the items of your search and get a new, personalized one.



Conclusion.

So don’t hesitate and just start your search for a more interesting study next semester.
